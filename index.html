Blue Button Trust Bundle

A collection of trust anchors enabling secure exchange to third parties on behalf of patients

During this pilot phase, this page is being managed by the Automate Blue Button patient community, a voluntary group of personal health service providers that are working together to accelerate patient engagement in support of better health.

"ABBI" is an ONC-sponsored project that aims to make it easy for patients to conveniently receive copies their own health information. It has its origins in the Blue Button initiative that first liberated health information for millions of veterans, military personnel and Medicare beneficiaries.

This page is meant for healthcare providers, vendors and personal health services that wish to use Direct Project secure messaging to support their patients and customers.
